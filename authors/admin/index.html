Junjie Ye (in Chinese: 叶俊杰) received his B.Eng. degree in mechanical engineering from Tongji University, Shanghai, China, with Excellent Graduate Honor in 2020. He is currently pursuing M.Sc. degree in mechanical engineering at Tongji University, Shanghai, China, under the supervising of Prof. Changhong Fu. His research interests include visual object tracking, deep learning, and robotics.
